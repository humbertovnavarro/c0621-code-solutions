Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong is a series of video games featuring the adventures of a gorilla called Donkey Kong, conceived by Shigeru Miyamato in 1981. The franchise consists of mainly of two game genres, but also includes additional spin-off titles of various genres.
The Games of the first genre are mostly single-screen platform/action puzzle types, featuring Donkey Kong as the opponent in an industrial construction setting.
